PRIVACY POLICY

How we protect and handle your information

1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to WATCH ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered shopping discovery platform.

Important Note

WATCH is a membership-based platform that provides AI-powered search and discovery features. We do not sell products directly - we redirect users to designer websites for purchases. We only offer memberships to access our exclusive AI-powered features.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, and profile preferences when you create an account
  • Membership Details: Subscription information, payment details, and membership tier
  • Search Preferences: Brands, styles, price ranges, and shopping preferences you set
  • Communication: Messages, feedback, and support requests you send to us

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform, search queries, and feature usage
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, and IP address
  • Cookies and Tracking: Information stored on your device to improve your experience
  • Analytics: Anonymous data about platform performance and user behavior

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Designer Websites: When you click through to designer sites, they may share information about your visit
  • Social Media: If you connect your social media accounts, we may receive profile information
  • Payment Processors: Confirmation of successful payments and subscription status

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

3.1 Core Platform Functions

  • Provide AI-powered search and discovery features
  • Personalize your shopping recommendations
  • Manage your membership and account
  • Process payments and subscriptions
  • Send you relevant deal alerts and updates

3.2 Platform Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our AI algorithms
  • Develop new features and enhance existing ones
  • Optimize platform performance and user experience
  • Conduct research and analytics

3.3 Communication

  • Send important account and membership updates
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Notify you about new features and exclusive deals

4. INFORMATION SHARING

We Do Not Sell Your Personal Information

WATCH does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.1 When We May Share Information

  • Designer Websites: When you click through to make a purchase, we may share basic information to track successful referrals
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us operate our platform (hosting, payment processing, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

4.2 Aggregated Data

We may share anonymous, aggregated data with partners and designers to help improve the shopping experience for all users.

5. DATA SECURITY

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication measures
  • Secure payment processing
  • Regular backups and disaster recovery procedures

6. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

6.1 Access and Control

  • Access, update, or delete your account information
  • Download a copy of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Control cookie preferences
  • Request data portability

6.2 Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time. We will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we need to retain certain information for legal or legitimate business purposes.

7. COOKIES AND TRACKING

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and login status
  • Analyze platform usage and performance
  • Personalize your experience
  • Provide relevant content and recommendations

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. See our Cookies Policy for more details.

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

WATCH is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our platform
  • Sending you an email notification
  • Displaying a prominent notice on our platform

Your continued use of WATCH after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
